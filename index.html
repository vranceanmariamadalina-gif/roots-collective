Team Workshops
Hands-on, heart-first.

Immersive team-building where cooking becomes a way to connect, reflect, and collaborate — without slides, icebreakers, or small talk that goes nowhere.

Each experience is intentionally designed using principles of adult learning and behavioral change, brought to life through facilitation tools like:

StorytellingLEGO® Serious Play®Liberating StructuresReflection cardsDesign thinking
Part storytelling, part gathering, all connection.

Carefully designed evenings built around a story, a question, or a theme. Each invites guests to slow down, cook, eat, and reflect together — turning a shared meal into a collective experience of curiosity and belonging.

We map a cuisine to your team's culture, explore fusion ingredients, and source everything together. Every dish is co-created to reflect your story, your values, and your flavor.

The arc of an evening

How it works

1

Begin together

We ground the group — setting intentions, opening space for curiosity, and introducing the theme that connects everyone in the room.

2

Create & explore

Through guided activities and reflection, we uncover personal and collective "layers" using creative tools, conversation, and gentle facilitation.

3

Connect & reflect

We share insights, stories, and discoveries in dialogue — finding meaning in the experiences and networks that shape who we are.

4

Close & carry forward

Every session ends with a grounded conversation, leaving the group feeling closer — and with something to take back to the work.
